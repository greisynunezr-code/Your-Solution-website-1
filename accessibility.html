Accessibility Statement

We want everyone to be able to use our website and reach our team. Here’s our commitment and how to get help.

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Your Solution Cooling & Appliance Repair is committed to making our website accessible to as many people as possible, including those who use assistive technologies. We believe everyone deserves an easy way to book the appliance repair help they need.

1. Our commitment

We strive to follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, as a practical standard for making web content more accessible to people with a wide range of abilities.

2. Measures we take

  • Clear, readable typography and strong color contrast;
  • Descriptive text alternatives (alt text) for meaningful images;
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation and visible focus states;
  • Responsive layouts that work across phones, tablets, and desktops;
  • Semantic structure (headings, lists, and labels) to support screen readers.

3. Compatibility

Our website is designed to work with current versions of major browsers (such as Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge) and with commonly used assistive technologies. Using the most up-to-date software gives the best experience.

4. Known limitations

Despite our efforts, some content may not yet be fully accessible. We are continually working to identify and resolve issues. If you encounter a barrier, please let us know — your feedback helps us improve.

5. Need help or have feedback?

If you have trouble accessing any part of our website, or you’d prefer to arrange service by phone, we’re glad to help directly:

  • Phone: (305) 429-4466
  • Email: help@yoursolutionrepair.com

Please tell us the page and the issue you experienced, and we’ll do our best to provide the information or service you need in an accessible way.

6. Ongoing effort

Accessibility is an ongoing commitment. We review our website periodically and make improvements as part of our regular updates.
