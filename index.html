07 — FAQ

Questions, answered.

What areas do you service?
We mainly service Miami-Dade and Broward counties, plus surrounding areas extending up to West Palm Beach. If you're nearby and not sure, give us a call at (305) 429-4466 and we'll let you know.
Do you repair refrigerators and freezers?
Yes — refrigeration is our core specialty. We handle units that won't cool, leaks, frost buildup, ice maker problems, noisy compressors and thermostat failures for both home and commercial units.
Do you work on sealed-system refrigerator issues?
Yes. Sealed-system repair is part of our confirmed service offerings. We diagnose the issue first and give you a clear, upfront quote before any work begins.
Do you service high-end and luxury appliance brands?
We service many premium and high-end appliance brands, including Sub-Zero, Viking, Bosch and more. Share your brand and model when you book so we arrive prepared with the right approach.
Do you offer same-day or emergency service?
Yes. We offer same-day appointments where available and after-hours coverage for urgent breakdowns — especially important for refrigeration, where time means spoiled inventory.
